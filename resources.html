Useful Resources

General Sites
  • The UK government's Equality Hub has various articles and reports on various topics related to diversity and inclusion.
  • Diversity Resources provides training and have an inclusive events calender.
  • Right To Behas a mission to build safe, inclusive public spaces by transforming the culture that perpetuates harassment and discrimination.
  • Inclusive Language Guidance from The University of Leeds is a useful overview of inclusive language in communications.
LGBTQ+ Organisations
  • Stonewall stands for lesbian, gay, bi, trans, queer, questioning and ace (LGBTQ+) people everywhere.
  • Gendered Intelligence offers support to young people, families, parents, and carers as well as organisations and professionals who wish to be more inclusive of trans, non-binary and gender questioning people in their settings.
Disability Organisations
  • The Disability Union aim to dismantle barriers and build a future of inclusivity.
